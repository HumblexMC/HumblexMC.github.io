About the server

Humblex. Originally named as PrivateTestingCraft,OceanCraft,WarriorWorld. All started when AxelTWC joined a server with the name "World of OreCraft", he wanted to unite the community after its closure.

Although the expectations were not met, however a new wave of community started joining in and slowly with moviation, he created numerous servers with a different concept.

IP: mc.humblex.net | Versions: 1.8-1.20.x in Java
