The Origin
of Humblex Phoenix Edition

500 Years Ago , Earth was fully infected by a horde of unknown hostiles, our people then decided to colonize planet Phoenix. Fast Forward years later, we successfully colonized the planet. But time is ticking, we start to run out of resources.

Therefore, a group of scientist has invented time travelling portal and spaceship for brave warriors to teleport back to Earth at 500 years ago to bring resources back. Warriors could choose to fight for survival themselves or alone.

Be careful, no body really have a clue on what Earth looked like 500 years ago , there are no buildings , you are responsible for your own life and your own accommodation.

There will be a Village when you land on Earth and they will need your help, it appears that there are infected mobs. Good luck on your journey to Humblex: Phoenix Edition.
